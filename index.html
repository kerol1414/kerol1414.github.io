Armored Vehicle Encyclopedia

Exploring the engineering and history of steel giants.

M1A2 Abrams

The M1 Abrams is a third-generation American main battle tank. It is highly mobile, designed for modern armored ground warfare, and features Chobham composite armor and a powerful multi-fuel turbine engine.

Tiger I (Panzer VI)

A heavy German tank of World War II, known for its thick armor and the lethal 8.8 cm KwK 36 gun. While feared on the battlefield, it was complex to engineer and maintain.

Leopard 2A7

Developed by Krauss-Maffei, the Leopard 2 is the backbone of many European armies. It is widely regarded for its balance of firepower, protection, and high-speed maneuverability.
